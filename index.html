THE WORLD AFTER THE BLAST

A Planet Rewritten by Radiation

Nuke World takes place in an alternate future where nuclear fallout didn’t end civilization — it mutated it.
Skyscrapers became arenas. Deserts glow at night. Energy pulses through the ground like a heartbeat.
Everything looks like it's about to fall apart, but somehow it's still alive, you know? Warning lights flicker, sirens echo in the distance, and every zone looks like it could collapse at any moment.
This is not a clean future.
It’s powerful, loud, and unforgiving.

HEROES & ENEMIES 

Mutations Decide Everything

Rhino

This guy's basically a walking tank - he doesn't do subtle, just smashes through whatever's in front of him.

Rooster

Unpredictable and aggressive. Thrives on chaos and sudden attacks.

Chameleon

A master of adaptation. Shifts tactics constantly, never playing the same way twice.

Armadillo

Defensive powerhouse. Turns endurance into a weapon.

Captain Fox

This guy's all about tactics - radiation made him sharp, and he's always thinking three moves ahead of everyone else.

BATTLEFIELD DYNAMICS

The Grid Is the Battlefield

The game unfolds across a wide, multi-reel grid that feels more like a combat zone than a board.
Elements align, react, and trigger chain effects that shift the flow of battle.
Crazy stuff can happen out of nowhere on the battlefield - you've gotta think fast or you're toast.
Things are constantly shifting around - blink and you might miss something important.
